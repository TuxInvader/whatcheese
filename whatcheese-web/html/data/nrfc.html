NGiNX release first Cheese

That's right the folks behind the technology that drives the worlds busiest websites (including this one), have released thier first cheese. The product in question is a rather squishy Brie with good mouth feel and notes of mushrooms and anchovie. The cheeky guys and girls at NGINX have named it "Brief Ncounter" due to it's limited availability, and mild laxative effects.

Industry experts are quoted as saying that this cheese is just a rebranding of a Somerset cheese which was briefly sold in Tesco and Marks & Sparks last year. The cheese was removed from the shelves following a health scare.
